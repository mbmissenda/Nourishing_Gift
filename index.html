Reheating with Love

For the best experience, follow these gentle reheating methods:

  • Soups & Chili: Add 1/2 cup broth to a sauce pan and add the frozen cube. Reheat slowly over medium heat.
  • Meatloaf & Hash: Place in an oven-safe dish. Add 1 tbsp water, cover, and bake at 350°F until warmed through.
  • Grains & Sides: Add 1-2 tbsp of water or broth, cover tightly, and microwave for 2-3 minutes to steam.
